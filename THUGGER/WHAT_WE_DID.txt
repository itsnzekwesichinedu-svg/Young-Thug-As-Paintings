Interaction decision
- Kept the gallery cards as 3D cards, but changed the behavior so the whole card no longer flips when tapped.
- Chose a clearer high-end gallery pattern:
  - Click or tap the image to open the full-size artwork in a full-screen lightbox.
  - Hover, click, or tap the small Info button to flip the card and read the literature.
  - Click the literature side itself to return to the artwork.

Why this is better
- Double tap can conflict with mobile browser zoom behavior.
- Separating image zoom from literature reading makes the gallery easier to understand.
- Visitors can admire the 1080 x 1080 image without accidentally flipping the card.

- Open index.html in a browser.
- Tap or click an artwork image to view it large.
